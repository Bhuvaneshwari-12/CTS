Community Bulletin

This weekend's community picnic brought families and friends together at Riverside Park for a day of fun, games, and delicious food. The sunny weather and live music created the perfect atmosphere for relaxing and reconnecting.

Local vendors set up stalls offering handmade crafts, organic produce, and tasty treats. Children enjoyed the face painting station and relay races, while adults participated in trivia contests and talent shows.

A heartfelt thank you to all the volunteers who made this event possible. Organizers are already planning next month's "Green City Cleanup" initiative, aimed at promoting environmental awareness and sustainability.

Stay updated through the community portal for more upcoming events, announcements, and ways to get involved. Together, we make our neighborhood vibrant and strong.
